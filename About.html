About Berserk Hub

Your ultimate destination for everything related to the Berserk universe.

Our Mission

At Berserk Hub, we aim to provide fans with a comprehensive resource for the Berserk series, including detailed timelines, character analyses, and the latest news. We are passionate about the world created by Kentaro Miura and strive to celebrate its legacy.

Who We Are

We are a team of dedicated fans and creators who have come together to share our love for Berserk. Our goal is to foster a community where fans can connect, share their thoughts, and explore the depths of this incredible story.

Join Us

Whether you're a long-time fan or new to the series, we invite you to join our community. Share your insights, participate in discussions, and stay updated with the latest news and events.
